My favourite band.

Intro

Judah and The Lion is my favourite band. It is a folk and rock band. Judah and the Lion is a Nashville three-man band formed in 2011. Its band members met in Belmont University from where they formed the band. The band released their first album 'First Fruit' in 2012. They later on went on to release other albums which did really well in Billboard charts and other music platforms. Their hit song 'Take it all back' became #1 on U.S.alternative songs charts in 2016. I love Judah and the Lion because of their genre of music(folk and rock). I mean the banjo really gets me. I also love them for their sweet, catchy, sometime uplifting lyrics. Members of the band

Originally the band started with four members, but one dropped out. Now there are three members in the band.

  1. JUDAH AKERS:

    Judah is the vocalist and guitarist of the band. He was born on 15th September 1990 in Cookeville Tennessee. He was a baseball player back in University before forming the band. He was also a basketball player.

  2. BRIAN MACDONALD:

    Brian is the Mandolin player and vocalist of the band. He was born on 24th November 1992 in Nashville Tennessee. He was studying mandolin back in Belmont University where he and the rest met and formed the band.

  3. NATE ZUERCHER:

    Nate is the Banjo player and vocalist of the band. Nate was born on 30th November 1991 in Colorado. He was studying the Banjo when they met in Belmont University.
