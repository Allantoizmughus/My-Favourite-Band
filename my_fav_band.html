Members of the band

Originally the band started with four members, but one dropped out. Now there are three members in the band.

  1. JUDAH AKERS: Judah is the vocalist and guitarist of the band. He was a baseball player back in University before forming the band.
  2. BRIAN MACDONALD: Brian is the Mandolin player and vocalist of the band. He was studying mandolin back in Belmont University where he and the rest met and formed the band.
    3. NATE ZUERCHER: Nate is the Banjo player and vocalist of the band. He was studying the Banjo when they met in Belmont University
